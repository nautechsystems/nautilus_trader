APPLICABLE WORK: `ecgfp5` reference implementation in Rust from the
`pornin/ecgfp5` repository, providing the Goldilocks field `Fp = 2^64 - 2^32 + 1`,
the quintic extension `GF(p^5)`, and the `ecgfp5` elliptic curve. Used as the
implementation reference for the `field/` and `curve/` modules under
`src/signing/` and as the source of test vectors reproduced under `test_data/`
to verify equivalence.
